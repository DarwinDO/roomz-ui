# Competitor Benchmarking Report

## 1. Airbnb (The Gold Standard)
- **Strengths**:
    - **Immersive Imagery**: Listings always lead with a 5-photo mosaic header. Reviewers mention "feeling like they are there".
    - **Soft UI**: Heavy use of `border-radius: 12px` or higher. Minimal borders, heavy use of soft shadows.
    - **Trust**: "Guest Favorites" and "Superhost" badges are visually distinct (often unique icons/colors).
- **Takeaway for Roomz**: Adopt the "Photo Grid" header instead of a carousel/single hero for desktop. Use warmer, softer shadows.

## 2. Flatfox (Swiss Efficiency)
- **Strengths**:
    - **Clarity**: Very structured data presentation.
    - **Map Integration**: Search results often paired directly with map pins.
    - **Typography**: Clean, neutral, high contrast.
- **Takeaway for Roomz**: Improve the "Amenities" grid readability using clarity from Flatfox.

## 3. iTro (Local Context)
- **Strengths**:
    - **Price Focus**: Price is usually the largest element.
    - **Density**: Shows many options quickly.
- **Takeaway for Roomz**: Ensure price and location are unmissable, as these are top user priorities in this market.

## Synthesis: Key Patterns to Adopt
1.  **"Cozy" Card Design**: Larger images, rounded corners (2xl), warm background for info area.
2.  **Trust Badges**: Promote "Verified Landlord" to a top-level visual element, not just a small tag.
3.  **Color Psychology**: Move away from Neon Blue. Use Deep Navy (Trust) and Warm Beige/Sand (Cozy).
